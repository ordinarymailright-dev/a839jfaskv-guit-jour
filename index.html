My Guitar Journal

• First lesson was on February 13
• Learned fundamentals of the guitar, technique, and musicality
• Found many composers/teachers like Carcassi, Giuliani, Tarrega and others
• Started getting into the music world as a whole and developing my sense of taste/personality
• Started with simple voice lines and melodies, ended with Chi Mai - a movie soundtrack arranged for guitar

First Year 2024

• Solidified my technique and developed a sense of musical style
• Started to love and appreciate many composers and teachers like Barrios, Sagreras, and Dilermando Reis
• Played through a more serious repertoire, ending with a piece by Dilermando Reis - Se Ela Perguntar

Second Year 2025

The year has just begun, so I have some plans set for it:
• Strengthen my technique so that I can pick up majority of the pieces I stumble upon and play by myself
• Continue developing my musical style and find even more of great composers/arrangers
• By the end of the year, try to play one of my role model pieces (Capriccio Arabe, Un Sueno En La Floresta, Standchen)
