My Guitar Journey

First Year

My very first year of playing guitar. I started recording my pieces in may. I've played through many well known teachers like Carcassi, Juliani, and Sagreras, slavic folk songs, and some more niche authors as well. This year was me learning the fundamentals of guitar.

Second Year

My development year as a guitarist. I started playing more serious pieces, learning music theory, and finding more composers. It was the year when classical guitar became my full passion. Many pieces I learned will be in my repertoire for years, ranging from famous pieces like Lagrima and Romance, and the pieces I deeply admire like Julia Florida and El Dia Florece Contigo.

Third Year

Coming soon. My plans are to learn more advanced pieces and build my technique so in the future I can play any piece I want.

My guitar journey 2023-recent. Website is updated as time moves on.
